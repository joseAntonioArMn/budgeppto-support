FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

Before reaching out to our support team, please check our FAQs section below. You might find answers to common queries here:

  1. How do I set up my monthly budget in Budgeppto?
    To set up your monthly budget, navigate to the "Budget" view and enter your desired monthly spending limit.
  2. How can I add my expenses as fixed in Budgeppto?
    You can add your expenses as fixed or daily from the same view. Simply enter the expense details, and if it's a fixed expense, activate the switch provided.
  3. How do I input daily expenses in Budgeppto?
    To add daily expenses, use the same expense input view. Enter the expense details, and if it's a daily expense, leave the switch deactivated.
  4. Why am I receiving notifications about my daily spending limit?
    Budgeppto sends daily notifications to help you stay within your daily spending limit. You can adjust or disable these notifications in the app settings.
